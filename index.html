Digital Addiction

When screen time starts controlling life

What is Digital Addiction?

Meaning

Uncontrolled and excessive use of digital devices.

Devices

Mobiles, laptops, tablets, gaming consoles.

Causes

Social media, gaming, online videos.

Impact on Students

Mental

Stress, anxiety, reduced focus.

Physical

Eye strain, poor sleep, headaches.

Academic

Lower concentration and performance.

Healthy Digital Habits

Time Limits

Fix daily screen-time limits.

Breaks

Follow the 20-20-20 rule.

Offline Life

Sports, reading, hobbies.
